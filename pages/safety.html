General Safety Measures During Disasters

  • Stay informed about the latest warnings and evacuation orders.
  • Prepare an emergency kit with essential supplies.
  • Know evacuation routes and designated shelters.
  • If indoors, stay away from windows, doors, and outside walls.
  • If outdoors, move to higher ground away from flood-prone areas.

Earthquake Safety

  • Drop, Cover, and Hold On: Get under a sturdy desk or table and hold on until the shaking stops.
  • Stay away from windows and outside walls.
  • If you are in bed, hold on and protect your head with a pillow.

Wildfire Safety

  • Stay informed about fire warnings and evacuation orders.
  • If advised to evacuate, do so immediately.
  • Close all windows and doors.
